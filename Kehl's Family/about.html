About Kehl's Family Boating Center

Over 40 Years of Maritime Excellence

Since 1978, Kehl's Family Boating Center has been a staple of the Lindenhurst, NY, community. As a family-oriented business, we've spent over five decades helping local boaters navigate the waters of Long Island with confidence. Our long-standing membership in the New York Marine Trades Association reflects our commitment to industry standards and our passion for the local boating lifestyle.

Your Premier Destination for Boat Sales

Whether you are looking to purchase a brand-new vessel or searching for the perfect value in a pre-owned model, our sales team is here to guide you. We specialize in matching families with boats that fit their lifestyle and budget.

  • New Boats: Featuring the latest models with cutting-edge technology.
  • Pre-Owned Boats: Rigorously inspected vessels that offer quality and reliability at an approachable price point.

Full-Service Care & Secure Storage

At Kehl's, our relationship with you doesn't end when you leave the dock. We are a full-service center dedicated to keeping your boat in peak condition. From routine professional boat services to keep your engine humming, to secure boat storage solutions that protect your investment during the off-season, we provide the peace of mind you need to focus on what matters most: enjoying your time on the water.
