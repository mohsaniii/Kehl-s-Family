Winter Boat Services

  • Outboard winterization: change engine oil and filter, change lower unit oil, run engine on fresh water and fogging mixture, check engine temperature and oil pressure, check for leaks, grease all fittings, remove prop and grease prop shaft, add fuel stabilizer to fuel tank, turn off battery switch.
  • Freshwater winterization — Drain water tank, connect water system to non-toxic antifreeze, & winterize.
  • Winterize head system
  • Shrink-wrap service — Includes canvas removal, store boat, set up frame for shrink wrap.
  • Shrink-wrap boats at residences
  • Haul, powerwash hull and outboard bracket, acid wash waterline and outboard bracket
  • Winter storage at our secure facility
  • Boat transport to home, yard, etc.
