New and Pre-Owned Boat Sales in Lindenhurst

Located on the south shore of Long Island in Lindenhurst, Kehl's Family Boating Center has sold and serviced new and used boats since 1982.

We are an authorized, exclusive dealer for Crownline and Sea Pro boats, and certified for Suzuki outboards. Kehl's Family Boating is family owned and operated, and our crew is dedicated to taking care of all your boating needs.
